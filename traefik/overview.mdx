title: Overview
---

Dokploy provides a simple way to manage your traefik configuration.

## Create

automatically when you create a new `application` it will create a new traefik configuration for each application.

## Manage

You have two ways to manage your traefik configuration.

- Go to `/dashboard/traefik` route and you will see a filesystem view of all traefik configuration files.
- Go to your application and click on `Advanced` tab, you will see at the end of the page a `Traefik` section, click on it and you will see a the configuration file.
