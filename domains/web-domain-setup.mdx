## Introduction

This section will cover the basic setup of a main domain.

## Requirements

First off is required to have a domain purchased.

You can use the following domains providers to purchase a domain, they are around between 2$ and 20$/year.

- [Namecheap](https://www.namecheap.com/domains/)
- [Name.com](https://www.name.com/)
- [GoDaddy](https://www.godaddy.com/)
- [Domain.com](https://www.domain.com/)


## Transfer Setup

Our suggest when you buy a domain is transfer your domain to Cloudflare.

this give advanced security, protection and advanced tools.

Here is a guide to transfer your domain to Cloudflare.

- [Transfer Domain to Cloudflare](https://developers.cloudflare.com/registrar/get-started/transfer-domain-to-cloudflare/)

## DNS Setup

We will use [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/) as our DNS provider, since provides the most secure and reliable DNS provider, but you can use any DNS provider.

Log in to your account and go to `Websites` -> `Click on your domain` -> `DNS` -> `Records`.

Then click on `Add record` and select `A`. 

In name field type in your domain name and in value field type in your IP address.

Example: let's suppose your domain is `dokploy.com` and your IP address is `1.2.3.4`.

and you want your panel be under `web.dokploy.com`, then type `web` in name field and `1.2.3.4` in value field and click on `Save`.

So your final record will be `web.dokploy.com`.

## Dokploy Panel Setup

Go to to your panel and log in with your credentials and go to `/dashboard?tab=settings` route and go to Server domain and set the exact same domain that you set in Cloudflare, click save give them like 20 seconds and go to `https://web.dokploy.com`.
