Dokploy is a simple, stable and easy to use deployment solution for your applications.

You can think `Dokploy` as an alternative OSS to Heroku, Vercel, Netlify, and many others.

It is designed to be as simple as possible, and to make it easy to deploy and manage your applications.

It is built on top of  [Docker](https://www.docker.com/) and [Traefik](https://traefik.io/), and it is designed to be as simple as possible, and to make it easy to deploy and manage your applications.
