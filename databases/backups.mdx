---
title: Backups
---


You can backup your databases using the `Backup` tab, and it will ask you.

- Select the destination S3 bucket, you can configure in `/dashboard/settings/destinations` route.
- Database name
- Schedule Cron
- Prefix of where you want to store the backups in your Bucket.
- Enabled or not (Default is enabled)


If you are not sure the information you entered is correct, you can click on `Test` button, it will trigger a test backup and it will show you the result in the bucket you selected.
